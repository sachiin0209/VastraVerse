Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cultural dressing platform

What is the Cultural Dressing Virtual Photoshoot?

It's an AI-powered platform that allows users to visualize themselves in traditional Indian attire using a single photo. By leveraging Generative AI and pose estimation, it creates hyper-realistic images where the user appears dressed in authentic cultural outfits.

How does the technology work?

We use a combination of pose estimation, GAN-based image synthesis, and Stable Diffusion with ControlNet. This enables precise clothing alignment, texture mapping, and realistic rendering of outfits on user-submitted photos.

What is the Festival & Ritual Guide Chatbot?

It's an interactive chatbot that educates users about Indian festivals, rituals, and traditional dress codes. It provides personalized suggestions based on regions, religions, and occasions, making the experience both informative and engaging.

Do I need special hardware or apps to use this platform?

No. The platform is fully web-based and optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile browsers—no downloads or installations required.

Is my photo stored or used anywhere else?

User privacy is a top priority. Photos are processed securely and either discarded after processing or stored temporarily with full user consent. We follow strict data protection policies.

Can I download or share my virtual photoshoot images?

Yes! After your virtual transformation is complete, you can download the image or share it on social media directly through the platform.

Which cultural attires are available?

We offer a growing collection of traditional outfits from various Indian states and communities, including sarees, lehengas, sherwanis, dhotis, turbans, and more. We continuously expand our wardrobe library to include more regions and customs.

Is this platform only for Indian users?

Not at all! It's designed for anyone interested in exploring and appreciating Indian culture—whether you're a student, traveler, diaspora member, or cultural enthusiast from around the world.

What makes this different from a costume filter app?

Unlike typical filters, our platform uses advanced AI for accurate body mapping, fabric simulation, and cultural context. It's not just about dressing up—it's about learning and engaging with heritage in a meaningful way.

Can this be used for education or tourism?

Absolutely. Our platform serves as a cultural gateway for schools, museums, tourism boards, and events. It promotes digital heritage, virtual experiences, and cultural literacy.
